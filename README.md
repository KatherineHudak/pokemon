This is the data for all Pokémon up to Gen 9.

**pokemon_attributes.csv **
List of Pokémon by name, with name, species, weight(kg),height(m) , male(%), female(%)

pokemon_list.csv
List of Pokémon and their ndex number

pokemon_regional_forms.csv
List of Pokémon with regional forms. Includes Pokémon name and the regional forms type and ability

pokemon_type.csv

Pokémon by name and ndex with primary and secondary type.

pokemon_stats.csv

Pokemon ndex, name, form, total, hp,attack,deffense, special attack, special defense and speed

** pokemon_abilities **
Pokemon ndex, name and ability

**pokemon_atler_form_type.csv**
List of Pokémon alternate forms and their type

pokemon_dex.csv
List of Pokémon and the dex number from each game
